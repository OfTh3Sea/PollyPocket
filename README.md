🎀 Polly Pocket Series
A vintage-inspired AI art series — seven compacts, seven versions of me.

🪞 About
This project reimagines the iconic Polly Pocket compact as a self-portrait medium. Starting with a reference photo of a real vintage Polly Pocket toy, I worked with Claude to craft image prompts for seven different themes — each one representing a different facet of my personality — then generated the final images in Google Gemini.

The result is a miniature self-portrait series that lives inside heart-shaped plastic compacts.

✨ The Seven Compacts

| Compact | Theme |
|---|---|
| 🧜‍♀️ Ocean Me | Mermaid world with coral, sea creatures, and a pearl |
| 👑 Princess Me | Pastel ballgown fantasy with a rainbow bedroom and garden |
| 💻 Techie Me | Silver lab packed with monitors, servers, and circuit boards |
| 🔭 Astrology Me | Antique study with celestial maps, crystals, and candles |
| 👽 Alien Me | Dark exobiology lab with a green-skinned figure and bioluminescent crystals |
| 🏥 Crazy Me | Polly's Quiet Care Centre, Ward 7 — patient doll included |
| 🌿 Cannabis Me | Lush botanical compact bursting with herbs and foliage |

🔀 The Workflow
1. Shared a vintage Polly Pocket reference image with Claude
2. Gave Claude a theme for each compact
3. Claude crafted detailed image prompts matching the vintage toy aesthetic
4. Generated each image in Google Gemini
5. Curated the final results

🛠️ Tools Used
- Claude (Anthropic) — prompt crafting and creative direction
- Gemini (Google) — AI image generation
